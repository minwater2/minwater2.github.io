Man Overboard

An Online Multiplayer Sailboat Racing Game

Man Overboard is an online multiplayer sailboat racing game. Players must sail their boat around the course as fast as possible while avoiding obstacles and other competitors. Real boat and wind data is used to provide an authentic sailing experience. This experience is augmented by arcade game elements including a player health system, collision physics, powerups and more. Round all the marks first to win the race but keep your crew members onboard. Who knows what’s lurking in these waters...

America's Cup Race Visualiser

A Visualiser that displays live data from America's Cup Match Races

America's Cup Race Visualiser displays the race data that is obtained from the data stream from the server and it also constantly updates till the stream finishes sending. The visualiser is consisted of three main modules. Data Receiver, Data Interpreter and lastly the Visualiser. These modules receives the data, converts the data and store the converted values to objects then visualises those objects.
