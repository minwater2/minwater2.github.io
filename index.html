WizARds (2020)

A Mixed Reality Local Multiplayer Game

WizARds is a mixed reality local multiplayer game where you can shoot at other players while also trying to catch a snitch that is controlled by the audiences playing on PC. WizARds was developed by me during the HIT Lab summer internship for Magic Leap with Unity Engine with the intention of making a fun game that invovles physical activity. Each magic leap player can shoot, dodge and deflect spells and the audiences control the snitch using their keyboard very similar to "Twitch Plays Pokemon"

My responsibilities in this game development:

  • Design and develop a mixed reality multiplayer game with spatial computing in mind
  • Learning a new technology/codebase
  • Handling network behaviors such as synchronized values, peer disconnections, RPCs, handling messages
  • Solving complicated problems creatively
  • Testing as I develop to exterminate bugs at earlier stages
  • Consulting my supervisor for improvements
